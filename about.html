Hello! I'm Mahmudul Bhuiyan from Bangladesh, currently a graduate student in the Department of Chemistry at Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science & Technology University (HSTU), one of the country's leading science and technology institutions.

My undergraduate program is specially focused on Bio-organic and Bio-inorganic Chemistry, chemical spectroscopy (UV-Vis, IR, NMR and Mass spectroscopy), Nanochemistry, Solid-state Chemistry, Analytical Chemistry, Coordination Chemistry, Natural Product and Medicinal Chemistry, and Green Chemistry.

Presently, my primary research interest lies in Nanozyme sensing, point-of-use kit development, and sustainable food safety. I have been actively working as a Research Assistant for more than two years at Dr. Md. Atikul Islam's Research Lab, contributing to numerous research projects.

With a strong interest in organic and inorganic nanozyme and analytical chemistry, I plan to pursue a Ph.D. and contribute meaningfully to the academic and scientific community.
